Our Mission

Connecting Tradition with Technology! 

‘E-panipuriikartz’ concept efficiently connects tradition with technology. The E-panipuriikartz has 3nozzles - each one dispenses different panipuri flavor. This, in turn, helps the customers to fill the puris with panipuri flavor on their own. 

What’s more, E-panipuriikartz is IoT enabled, mobile app operated which controls switching on/off, checks Water Level, Water Flow, Filling, Tampering Control, and also provides Live Report.
